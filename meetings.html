Western Arizona Economic Development District

Board Meeting Information

Per A.R.S. § 38-431.09(A) and Bylaws Section 8.2, it is the public policy of the District to conduct meetings openly and that notices and agendas for such meetings will contain such information as is reasonably necessary to inform the public of the matters to be discussed or decided. Toward this end, the District is in favor of open and public meetings.

Board Elections. Board members shall be elected at least annually by the voting representatives of member organizations. The Board Elections are conducted in January.

Annual Meeting. Board of Directors shall set the Annual Meeting each June and announce the date, time, and location of the Annual Meeting.

Regular Board of Directors meetings are conducted four times per calendar year on the last Tuesday of January, April, June, and October.

General Board of Directors (including At-Large members) meetings are conducted twice per calendar year on the last Tuesday of March and September.

Standing and Special Committees shall meet on their Committee Chairman’s call with notification to the Committee Members and Executive Director at least seven (7( days before the meeting. An agenda specifying the subject of all meetings shall accompany the notice.

Board meeting locations rotate alphabetically to each county.

Board meetings are always open to the public and are accessible.

Remote meeting participation is always available.
