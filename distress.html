Footnote:

Economic development districts are designated in regions where the 24-month unemployment rate or the per capita income is less than 80% of the national average (i.e., the threshold calculation).

Thus, the District has the paradoxical mission of working itself out of a job by leveraging regional, state, and national partnerships to grow the number and diversity of employers who can create and grow quality jobs. We are not there yet, but with your help, we can get there!

We build connections with the people and communities in La Paz, Mohave, and Yuma counties.
